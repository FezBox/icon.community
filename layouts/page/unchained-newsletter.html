ICON is a Cross-Chain Hub

The ICON Network, as a cross chain hub, provides a connection between different blockchain networks.

Our unique standard for enabling generic cross-chain messaging between heterogeneous blockchains is named xCall Service. The xCall service is one of the key features of ICON's Blockchain Transmission Protocol (BTP), a blockchain interoperability protocol that enables different blockchains to communicate with one another.
