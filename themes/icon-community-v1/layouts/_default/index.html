Decentralized, Fast, and Secure Interoperability

ICON's Blockchain Transmission Protocol (BTP) enables cross-chain communication between smart contract-enabled blockchains. BTP is fully trustless, which means your funds are SAFU. Start bridging your assets between ICON, Binance Smart Chain, NEAR Protocol, Moonriver, and more with Nexus – our easy to use bridging app powered by BTP.
